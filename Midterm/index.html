Background

Our mission is to revolutionize the mobility experience by designing assistive devices that empower users with greater independence and confidence. We are committed to creating products that not only support physical movement but also enhance the emotional and psychological well-being of our users. Through personalized design and complete customization in both form and function, we aim to provide mobility solutions that truly adapt to the unique needs and preferences of each individual, ensuring a more vibrant and fulfilling life.

Coming Soon: Connect

inStep Connect introduces a range of innovative features designed to enhance user support and improve safety and well-being. With real-time bio-informatics tracking, users can monitor vital health data and stay informed about their condition. In case of a fall, the app triggers an emergency response, instantly notifying caregivers or emergency services for swift assistance. Additionally, the app offers localized alarms, providing timely notifications based on the user's location for added security. These features work together to offer a more personalized and responsive support system, giving users greater peace of mind and better control over their health.
